Modern Dance

Modern dance is a dynamic and expressive dance style that breaks away from classical ballet rules. It allows dancers to explore movement freely and connect deeply with emotions.

In Art in Motion, modern dance encourages individuality, creativity and confidence. It helps dancers develop body awareness, fluidity and personal style.
